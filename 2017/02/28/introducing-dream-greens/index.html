Introducing Our New Retail Brand

We are thrilled to introduce our new retail brand…

Meet Dream Greens!

 

With this fun and inviting brand, we are setting a new culinary standard for flavor and freshness. Dream Greens are locally-grown, pesticide-free, non-GMO and offer consistent quality and availability year-round. Our blends of baby greens feature consumer favorites like baby kale and arugula as well as some spicier newcomers like ruby streaks and baby watercress. These are the same delicious greens that HydroFarms customers have been enjoying for years, but now with improved packaging and some new, exciting blends.

However Dream Greens are even more revolutionary than they may appear. Besides the paradigm-shifting way they are grown, Dream Greens packaging is also more sustainable than the traditional plastic clamshell. By losing the lid, we have saved 40% of the plastic in most packaged greens. Furthermore, the resealable plastic film has micro-perforations, which are tailored to the respiration rate of each green or blend, offering improved shelf life. Learn more on the Dream Greens Website.

We are excited to share this new journey with you! Please follow along with @LoveDreamGreens on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter and Pinterest and purchase Dream Greens at these retailers. Share your photos from the store to the kitchen with us using #LoveDreamGreens .
